History

We started our business in 1972. We had a rough start initially, but we later became a local hit in the Cleveland area.




About Patrick Thompson

Patrick Thompson was born on April 17th, 1954. He shared an interest in food from a young age. His mother often let him cook for his family. This desire to please others with food helped him become who his is today.



History of charity

Shortly after the Vietnam war ended in 1975, Patrick developed a "Vets eat free" sale, and this sale holds true today. 35% of proceeds until 1985 were given to military charity organizations. Today, we donate to St. Jude for the most part.
