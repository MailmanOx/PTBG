What we do

We provide a variety of baked goods for our customers to eat. We will bake privately for those who ask. We do birthday parties, weddings, meetings, and much much more.



Why we do it

We do what we do because we believe that the joy a good bagel brings should be shared with the world. We simply just want to make people happy. Futhermore, purchasing from the store will donate money to St. Jude.
